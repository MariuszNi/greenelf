Calculate Your Returns on a GreenElf Applicator Investment

Please get in contact with us and we can assist you to calculate your possible return on investment by comparing your current fertiliser top dressing rates to expected rates using the GreenElf Applicator at your nursery.

We have spreadsheet calculators and case studies at hand to help you get an understanding of what can be achieved, taking into factors such as dose rates, container size and application speeds and refill break times.

Many of our customers achieve rates between 25,000 and 30,000 per 8 hour working day, including breaks and refill times. You may be surprised to find that full payback on your investment may be possible after just 6 days of use!

Do not forget the other savings that can be provided by the GreenElf Applicator: eliminate fertilizer wastage with accurate dosing, less employee downtime due to easier and safer work and shot count tracking that can be used to better analyse and improve management of resources.
