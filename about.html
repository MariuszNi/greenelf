The GreenElf Applicator was originally developed by Ian Seton. Ian grew up in rural Australia where, if there is no ready-made solution to a problem, very often you have no option but to work it out yourself.

Now a family business run by Ken and Paul, GreenElf Works has a growing network of distributors and service centers around the world. They bring additional technical and customer service skills to support customers locally.

GreenElf Works is based in Sydney, Australia.
